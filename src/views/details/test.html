MoreThe post-apocalyptic sci-fi survival RPG game Earth:Revival is set in an alien-invaded world and centers around the core experience of open-world survival. It has undergone significant industry innovation and upgrades. Experience groundbreaking advancements with its "visual revolution", leading-edge "Effortless Play and Fair Spending Reform," a collaboratively crafted sci-fi narrative, and a unique exosuit combat system. Dive into the distinct "Artificial Island" building and social mechanics... Here, every player, regardless of their play-style preference, can finds something to engage with. Explore the futuristic world, engage in combat, and embrace any and all unconventional survival methods in "Earth: Revival." Free to explore, as long as you stay alive! [Survival Beyond 'Just Getting By'] Step into the shattered world of 2112, this time, not just "one man and his dog" anymore. You'll be accompanied by a variety of pets, including giant pandas and mythical creatures like the Kun. [Tech and Tactics] Utilize source codes, chips, and infusions to customize your high-tech gear. Pick your favorite exosuit and head into battle. [Quality Living in the Apocalypse] Owned island, build furniture as you like, and invite friends to your utopian hideaway. Embrace quality living even in the apocalypse. [Global Sci-Fi Collaborations] Top sci-fi minds all around the world come together to create this game. Renowned writers developed the storyline, Gundam mechanical master designer Shoji Kawamori(JP) designed the exosuits, and famous architect Ma Yansong(CN) crafted the furniture. [Effortless Play and Fair Spending] Strongholds System, simple click to collect resources, and craft portable items using renewable energy to do 3D print with ease. [Agile Guns Combat] Various fair PVP modes where skillful movement and precise shooting determine victory! [Nightlife Every Day] Clubbing and chess to hide-and-seek, different leisure activity to enjoy every day! [Fine to be alone, better to be whole] The "drift bottle" feature in the vast world helps social introverts connect. Cohabitation, squads, and guild systems ensure heartwarming encounters in the apocalypse. [Player-Driven Content] Creative Workshop mode, freely edit gameplay and build on Artificial Islands, and organize vehicle races. If you've got great ideas, bring them to life!
